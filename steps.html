Crafting Steps

Follow this seamless process to bring your custom furniture concepts to life.

1

Choose a Shop

Browse our verified network of skilled local craftsmen and furniture shops. Send them your exported design directly to receive quotes and begin the build process.

2

Choose Your Furniture

Select from our wide range of furniture pieces including chairs, tables, sofas, beds, cabinets, bookshelves, desks, and stools. Each foundation is designed for durability.

3

Select Materials

Choose from premium materials like narra wood, mahogany, steel, fabric, and leather. You can use a single material or combine different textures for specific parts.

4

Customize Details

Personalize your furniture with custom color palettes, specialized finishes, exact dimensions, and quantities. Add personal design notes to make the piece truly yours.

5

Export Design

Save your fully customized furniture design as a high-fidelity 3D model or image file. Keep it for personal reference or prepare it for professional crafting.
